Friends Modpack

A custom Minecraft modpack for Minecraft 1.21.1 on NeoForge.

Download CurseForge Modpack

Direct download link: https://filipjaruska.github.io/minecraft-mods/FriendsModpack.zip

CurseForge Installation

  1. Download CurseForge App
  2. Open the CurseForge App and click on "Minecraft" tab
  3. Click "Create Custom Profile" in the top right
  4. Click "Import" at the bottom
  5. Select the downloaded FriendsModpack.zip file
  6. Click "Play" to launch the modpack!
